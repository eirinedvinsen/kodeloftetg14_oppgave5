Fox Facts!

  • Foxes don’t hibernate, and aren’t bothered by the cold.
  • Foxes sleep in dens up to 8 ft deep.
  • Foxes can make over 40 different vocalizations. To put it into perspective, humans have 3 and 1/3 octaves. Foxes have five octaves that allow them to produce 40 different vocalizations.
  • The species name for red foxes is Vulpes vulpes
  • A group of foxes is called a Skulk.
